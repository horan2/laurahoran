Corbis

I feel really fortunate to have been able to cut my taxonomy teeth on the Corbis Controlled Vocabulary. It supports search for over 50 million images, spanning a wide variety of photography types: historical, breaking news, celebrity/entertainment, documentary, and commercial stock. Really, anything that can be photographed: from historic images of the civil rights movement to Roman frescos, to cheesy businessmen shaking hands and women laughing alone with salads. As a result, the scope of the taxonomy is anything that can be photographed. Which was both thrilling and incredibly daunting.
